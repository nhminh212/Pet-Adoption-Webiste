Who are we?

APL (A Pet Life) is a non-profit organization formed with a sole purpose of rescuing and providing stray and mistreated pets a home along with an opportunity to be loved and cared by a true pet-loving family.

We are still a small and new organization compares to other large, international organization out there but we believe we all share the same passion toward pets and goal to rescue as many pets as possible.

Our motive

We care deeply about pets in general and will do whatever it takes to be able to at least free them from their tortured living condition and offer a much deserving live up ahead.

We work to improve the welfare of pets by:

  • Ensuring that every pet is cared for properly and has a good home
  • Ending cruelty to pet animals
  • Raising standards for pet animals worldwide
  • Host fundraising festival to allow pets to interact with more people as well as people get to take a closer look at the pets
